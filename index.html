About Me

Hi, I’m Mohamed Adil, a Web Developer, App Developer, Game Developer, Creative Coder, UI/UX Designer, and Graphic Designer passionate about building modern and engaging digital experiences.

I graduated from Bath Spa University, where I developed multiple projects including interactive games, full-stack websites, and creative digital applications.

I have also worked with companies to design and develop professional websites tailored to their business needs.

My skill set includes HTML, CSS, JavaScript, UI/UX design, and graphic design, using tools like Figma to create clean, user-focused interfaces and visual content.

I enjoy turning ideas into functional, creative, and impactful digital solutions.
